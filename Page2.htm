Jakiro

Jakiro Abilities

Jakiro, the Twin Head Dragon, is a
ranged intelligence hero who utilizes
the powers of ice and fire with powerful
linear area spells. He can freeze enemies
in place with Ice Path, or slow them down
with Dual Breath.

Dual Breath

An icy blast followed by a wave of fire
launches out in a path in front of Jakiro.
The ice slows enemies, while the fire
delivers damage over time.


Ice Path

Creates a path of ice that stuns
and damages enemies that touch it.



Liquid Fire

Jakiro burns his enemies in an area of
effect with fire added to his attack,
while slowing their attacks.
Deals 75% damage to buildings.
